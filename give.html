"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

Where your gift goes

Sundays. Kids ministry. Worship. Outreach to our city. Mission partners around the world. Every gift is a vote of faith in what God is doing here.

Secure & private

All payments are processed through bank-grade encryption. We never store your card details.

Tax-deductible

New Season Christian Church is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit. All donations are deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

Annual giving statements are emailed each January.

Questions?

Email giving@newseason.church and we'll get back to you within one business day.

Other ways to give

Online is easiest. But not the only way.

If online giving isn't for you, here are three more ways to participate.

01 / Text

Text to give

Text GIVE to (555) 123-4567 from your phone. First-time setup is two minutes.

02 / In Person

Give Sunday

Giving boxes are at the back of the room every Sunday. Cash, check, or scan the QR code — your choice.

03 / Mail

By mail

Make checks payable to New Season Christian Church and mail to the church office.

Thank you

Every gift matters.

Whether it's $5 or $500 — you're part of the family that's making this work happen. We're grateful for you.
