Ministries

Find your people.

Six ministries. One family. Whatever season you're in — kid, college student, young mom, retired veteran — there's a space here that was made with you in mind.

01 — Kids

Where kids meet a real Jesus.

New Season Kids is a safe, joy-filled space where children encounter the love of God through age-appropriate teaching, worship, story, and play. From the nursery to fifth grade — every child is celebrated, every week.

Background-checked volunteers. Secure check-in. A space their parents are excited to bring them back to.

Ages
0–11 (Nursery → Grade 5)
When
Sundays · 9 & 11 AM
Where
Kids Wing, Main Campus
Lead
Pastor Mia Reyes
Pre-register your child
02 — Youth

Built for the middle years.

Middle and high schoolers becoming who God created them to be — together. Real questions, real friendships, real faith for the loudest, most-overlooked years.

Wednesdays are a midweek anchor: worship, teaching, small groups, and a lot of laughter.

Ages
12–18 (Grades 6–12)
When
Wednesdays · 7:00 PM
Where
The Loft
Lead
Pastor Marcus Hale
Join the youth list
03 — Young Adults

For the in-between years.

College students and twenty-somethings figuring out faith, work, identity, and what comes next. A space to ask big questions without easy answers — and find people walking the same road.

Thursday nights at the church café. Dinner together first, then teaching, then small groups in living rooms across the city.

Ages
18–30
When
Thursdays · 6:30 PM
Where
The Café + Home Groups
Lead
Pastor Sarah Okafor
Find a home group
04 — Women

Sisters walking in courage.

A community of women across every chapter of life — single, married, mothers, grandmothers, students, leaders — learning to live in step with the Spirit and unafraid of the road ahead.

Monthly gatherings, seasonal retreats, weekly bible studies, and friendships that last past Sunday.

Who
All women, all stages
When
Monthly · 2nd Saturday
Bible Study
Tuesdays · 9:30 AM & 7 PM
Lead
Pastor Mia Reyes

05 — Men

Brothers building faithful lives.

Men of every age learning what it looks like to live with honor, presence, and follow-through. Less performance, more substance. A space where it's safe to be the man you're actually trying to become.

Saturday morning breakfasts, small groups, and an annual retreat that's become legendary for early-morning runs and late-night fires.

Who
All men, all stages
When
Saturdays · 7:30 AM
Where
The Café
Lead
Pastor Daniel Reyes
Join the next breakfast
06 — Outreach

For our city. And beyond.

We believe the church doesn't exist for itself. Outreach is how we love our city out loud — serving neighbors, partnering with schools, walking with the unhoused, and sending teams to the nations.

Whether you have an hour a month or a heart that won't sit still, there's a place for you on a team.

Partners
