About New Season

A church built for the in-between.

We started in a living room with thirteen people, one folding table, and a stubborn belief that the local church is still God's plan A. We're still believing it.

Our Story

From a living room to a city-wide family.

In 2018, Pastor Daniel and his wife Mia gathered a few friends in their apartment to ask one question: what would it look like to plant a church where nobody had to pretend?

Seven years later, that question is still our compass. We've grown — but we haven't grown out of honesty. We're still a place where the burnt-out, the curious, the formerly-religious, and the deeply-faithful can sit shoulder to shoulder and call it home.

We believe seasons change. People change. But the love of God for ordinary people doesn't. That's the room we're trying to build.

What we hold to

Five values.
Quietly non-negotiable.

These aren't slogans on a wall. They're the way we make decisions when nobody's looking.

01

Jesus, plainly.

We preach Jesus. Not a brand, a politician, or a personality. The actual Jesus of the actual gospels — strange, kind, demanding, alive.

02

Honest rooms.

Pretending kills faith faster than doubt ever did. You don't have to perform here. We'd rather have a messy real than a polished fake.

03

Whole family.

Multi-generational, multi-ethnic, multi-story. Every age in the room, every background welcome at the table, every voice worth hearing.

04

Cities matter.

We're for our city. Not above it, not against it. We pray for it, serve it, and refuse to give up on it.

05

The long road.

Faithfulness over hype. Years of small obedience over a viral moment. The kind of church our grandkids will still want to be part of.

What we believe

The short version.

We hold to historic, orthodox Christianity — what the Church has believed across centuries and continents. Here's the heart of it.

▸

The Bible

The inspired, trustworthy Word of God — our final authority for faith and life.

▸

One God, three Persons

Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — eternally one, working together for our rescue.

▸

Jesus, fully

Fully God, fully human, crucified, risen, returning. The hinge of all history.
